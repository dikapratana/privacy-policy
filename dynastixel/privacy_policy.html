Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

Welcome to Dynastixel. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

Dynastixel is designed as a local-first application. We collect minimal information to provide the best gaming experience:

  • Profile Information: We allow you to choose a "Warrior Name" and a "Unique ID". This information is used solely to identify you within the game interface.
  • Game Progress: Settings, scores, and unit selections are saved to help you continue your progress.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

Our app includes features from the following third-party services which may collect information according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: We show advertisements to support the development of Dynastixel. Google may use your device's Advertising ID to show personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: When you purchase skins or other virtual items, the transaction is handled entirely by Google. We receive confirmation of the purchase but never see your payment details (such as credit card numbers).

3. Data Storage

Your game profile (Name and ID) and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this profile data to our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Dynastixel does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. All data provided is stored locally and is under the user's control.

5. Data Security

Since your data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your mobile operating system updated.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@dynastixel.com
