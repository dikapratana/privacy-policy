How to Delete Your Account and Data

In Dynastixel, we prioritize your privacy by storing all your game data, including your Warrior Name and progress, locally on your device. We do not maintain any data on our servers.

Steps to Delete Your Data

To permanently remove all data associated with Dynastixel, follow these steps:

  1. Clear App Cache and Data:
    • Open Settings on your Android/iOS device.
    • Go to Apps or Applications.
    • Find and select Dynastixel.
    • Tap on Storage.
    • Select Clear Data and Clear Cache. This will erase your name, unique ID, and all progress.
  2. Uninstall the Application:
    • Long-press the Dynastixel icon on your home screen.
    • Select Uninstall or Remove App.
Warning: Deleting app data is permanent. Since we do not store backups on our servers, your progress cannot be recovered once it is cleared from your device.
